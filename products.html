Our Products

ScalarLabs develops applied technologies for communication, safety, and risk reduction. Our flagship product is Vocari.

Flagship Product

Vocari

Real-Time Earbud Communication Software

Vocari is a privacy-focused, earbud-to-earbud communication utility designed to help people talk and listen in difficult acoustic environments. The system routes live voice audio between local range phones and connected earbuds.

Primary markets: aviation, industrial teams, accessibility support, live events, field operations, and OEM integration.
