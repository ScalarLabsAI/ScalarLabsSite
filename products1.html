Our Products

ScalarLabs develops applied technologies for communication, safety, and risk reduction. Our flagship product is Vocari.

Vocari

The Problem

Real-world conversation often breaks down in noisy environments: restaurants, aircraft cabins, factories, engine rooms, live events, public spaces, and accessibility-sensitive situations. Standard Bluetooth earbuds help isolate sound, but they do not let two nearby people communicate naturally through their own earbuds when background noise overwhelms normal speech.

Our Solution

Vocari is a privacy-focused, earbud-to-earbud communication utility designed to help people talk and listen in difficult acoustic environments. The current system routes live voice audio between nearby phones and connected earbuds, creating a low-friction communication bridge without relying on transcription as the primary experience.

The Value

  • For users: Clearer real-time conversation in noisy spaces using devices they already carry.
  • For accessibility: Practical hearing support for people who need cleaner, more controlled voice audio in challenging environments.
  • For partners: A scalable communication utility with OEM, accessibility, professional, aviation, industrial, and consumer deployment potential.

Druber (SafeRide Utility)

The Problem

Alcohol-impaired driving remains a major public safety and financial burden in the United States. In 2024, 11,904 people were killed in drunk-driving crashes — approximately one death every 44 minutes. Beyond the human toll, alcohol-related crashes generate tens of billions of dollars annually in economic damage, including medical costs, legal liability, lost productivity, property damage, insurance losses, and emergency response expenses.

Our Solution

Druber is a patent-pending, one-tap safe ride utility designed for direct integration into insurance carrier, transportation, hospitality, and public safety ecosystems. It allows users to pre-plan transportation before impairment occurs, then activate a safe ride home with minimal friction when needed. The platform is designed to reduce impaired-driving incidents, liability exposure, claims costs, and preventable fatalities.

The Value

  • Industry impact: Even modest reductions in impaired-driving incidents could translate into billions of dollars in avoided economic losses, reduced insurance claims, and fewer roadway fatalities annually.
  • For insurers: Reduced exposure to alcohol-related claims, litigation, punitive damages, fraud risk, and reputational harm, while improving member engagement and safety positioning.
  • For employers and venues: A practical risk-reduction tool for restaurants, bars, hospitality groups, event operators, and corporate environments where transportation safety and liability management matter.
  • For states and municipalities: Alignment with public safety initiatives, impaired-driving reduction programs, and potential support for federal highway safety funding initiatives including Sections 402 and 405(d).
