The Problem

Real-world conversation often breaks down in noisy environments: restaurants, aircraft cabins, factories, engine rooms, live events, public spaces, and accessibility-sensitive situations.

Standard Bluetooth earbuds help isolate sound, but they do not let two local range people communicate naturally through their own earbuds when background noise overwhelms normal speech.

Our Solution

Vocari is a privacy-focused, earbud-to-earbud communication utility designed to help people talk and listen in difficult acoustic environments.

The system routes live voice audio between local range phones and connected earbuds, creating a low-friction communication bridge without relying on transcription as the primary experience.

The Value

  • For users: Clearer real-time conversation in noisy spaces.
  • For accessibility: Practical hearing support in challenging environments.
  • For partners: Scalable deployment across OEM, aviation, industrial, and consumer markets.
