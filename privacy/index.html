Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ScalarLabs may collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit this website, contact ScalarLabs, apply for opportunities, or interact with ScalarLabs products, services, or demonstrations.

Depending on how you interact with ScalarLabs, we may collect:

  • Application information, such as resume materials or employment inquiry details.
  • Technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, referral source, and basic usage logs.
  • Product or research inquiry information voluntarily provided through email or forms.

2. How We Use Information

ScalarLabs may use information to:

3. Cookies and Analytics

The website may use basic cookies, server logs, or privacy-respecting analytics to understand site performance and usage. ScalarLabs does not need sensitive personal information for ordinary website browsing.

4. Information Sharing

ScalarLabs does not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help operate the website or process communications, with professional advisors, or when required to comply with law, protect rights, prevent fraud, or secure systems.

5. Data Retention

ScalarLabs retains information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer period is required for legal, security, business, or recordkeeping reasons.

6. Security

ScalarLabs uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No website or transmission method is perfectly secure.

7. Your Choices

You may contact ScalarLabs to request access, correction, or deletion of information you provided, subject to legal, security, and operational limits.

8. Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13, and ScalarLabs does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this website.

10. Changes to This Policy
