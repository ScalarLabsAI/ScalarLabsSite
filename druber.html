A patent-pending utility designed to reduce impaired-driving risk before the moment of decision.

Request Demo Contact Us

The Problem

Alcohol-impaired driving remains a major public safety and financial burden. Preventable crashes create human loss, insurance exposure, legal risk, property damage, and emergency response costs.

Our Solution

Druber gives users a low-friction way to pre-plan transportation before impairment occurs and activate a safe ride when needed.

The Value

  • For insurers: reduced claim and liability exposure.
  • For venues: practical risk reduction.
  • For states: alignment with impaired-driving prevention programs.

Partner Deployment

Druber is designed for insurers, transportation providers, hospitality groups, employers, events, states, and municipalities.
